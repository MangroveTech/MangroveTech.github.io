About us

In a world where social networking and instant messaging dominates how we communicate, why does email on smartphones continue to be so complicated?

Wave is the culmination of a two-year project to bring simplicity and speed to email, in an effort to make email on mobile devices work the way users want it to work.

Founded by previous employees of Yahoo and Alibaba, Just Mobile stretches the globe with offices in San Francisco and Beijing's Silicon Valley.
